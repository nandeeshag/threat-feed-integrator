What is Threat Intelligence Feeds?

In today's digital landscape, the continuous evolution of cyber threats and attacks poses significant challenges to organizations' cybersecurity efforts. To effectively defend against these dynamic threats, security professionals require a comprehensive, real-time, and adaptable threat intelligence solution.
The Threat Intelligence Integration Platform (TIIP) is a robust and innovative project designed to meet this need. TIIP serves as a centralized, automated, and intelligence-driven platform that aggregates, analyzes, and disseminates threat intelligence feeds, enhancing an organization's cybersecurity posture.
